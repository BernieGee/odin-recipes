Chilli Oil

Description


This is the spiciest situation
Suitable for putting on any meal!

Ingredients:

    1 cup chilli flakes
    1 cup rice bran oil
    1 tablespoon salt
    2 table spoons sichuan peppercorns
    cinnamon sticks, cloves, bay leaves, aromatics

Steps

  1. heat oil, aromatics and peppercorns on low for 20-30 minutes until chrispy
  2. Fill sterilized jar with chilli flakes
  3. Strain oil to remove aromatics
  4. Pour slightly cooled oil over chilli flakes so they bubble a bit and cook
  5. Add salt to help preserve and for flavour
  6. Once cooled a bit put on lid and let sit for a week or two so flavour develops
  7. add to any meal!
