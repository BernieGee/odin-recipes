Boiled Egg

Description


This is how to cook an egg!
Suitable for when you need a simple tasty snack.

Ingredients:

    1 Egg
    1 cup rice bran oil
    1 tablespoon salt
    Water

Steps

  1. Put egg in pot and cover with cold water
  2. Add salt
  3. Cook on high heat until water boils
  4. Once water is boiling turn down heat to half. Set timer for 4 minutes
  5. When time is up drain and rinse with cold water. This makes it easier to peel
  6. Peel and serve on toast with butter!
